R&B SONGWRITING STRUCTURE + EMOTIONAL VOCABULARY LIBRARY Song Writing
Project – Consolidated Pattern Source File

  ---------
  PURPOSE
  ---------

This document defines structural templates, lyrical frameworks, and
vocabulary banks for writing modern R&B songs.

Unlike aggressive genres such as deathcore, R&B prioritizes:

• emotional storytelling • melodic phrasing • sensual or intimate
vocabulary • smooth cadence and vowel flow

The goal is to generate emotionally coherent R&B lyrics quickly while
preserving musicality and narrative depth.

  ----------------
  CORE PRINCIPLE
  ----------------

Structure remains consistent. Story, emotion, and imagery change.

R&B songs rely heavily on emotional relatability and melodic phrasing
rather than complex rhyme structures.

  -------------------------
  VERSE WRITING FRAMEWORK
  -------------------------

Line 1 – Situation Line 2 – Emotional reaction Line 3 – Desire or
conflict Line 4 – Vulnerability

Example logic:

You called me late Said you couldn’t sleep Your voice was shaking Now
you’re pulling me deep

  ------------------
  CHORUS FRAMEWORK
  ------------------

The chorus expresses the emotional core of the relationship.

Format A – Emotional Hook

Statement Repetition Escalation Final emotional statement

Format B – Desire Hook

Confession Physical or emotional desire Repeat phrase Memorable closing
line

  ----------------
  BRIDGE PURPOSE
  ----------------

The bridge provides the emotional peak.

Possible themes:

• confession of love • realization of betrayal • moment of vulnerability
• emotional surrender

Example:

Maybe I was wrong Maybe I was blind But every time I run You pull me
back inside

  -------------------------------
  R&B EMOTIONAL VOCABULARY BANK
  -------------------------------

melodic singing.

  -----------------------
  VOCABULARY USAGE RULE
  -----------------------

R&B favors softer consonants and flowing vowels:

M N L W B

These sounds produce smoother melodic phrasing and emotional warmth
during singing.
