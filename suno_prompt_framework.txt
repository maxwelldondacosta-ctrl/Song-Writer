SUNO PROMPT CREATION FRAMEWORK Song Writing Project – Production Prompt
Guide

  ----------
  OVERVIEW
  ----------

This document defines the method for creating Suno prompts under 1000
characters that accurately translate finished lyrics into the intended
musical style.

The goal is to describe the musical DNA of the track clearly so the AI
produces consistent results.

Prompts must avoid artist names and focus on musical descriptors
instead.

  --------------------------
  1. DEFINE GENRE AND TONE
  --------------------------

Begin by describing the genre and emotional tone.

Example:

Aggressive modern deathcore with crushing breakdowns and dark emotional
intensity.

The genre sets the fundamental musical direction.

  ---------------------------
  2. DEFINE INSTRUMENTATION
  ---------------------------

Clearly describe the instruments and sound design.

Example:

Downtuned chugging guitars, blast beats, heavy double kick drums,
distorted bass.

The AI responds better to specific instrumentation cues.

  -------------------------
  3. DESCRIBE VOCAL STYLE
  -------------------------

Specify the vocal delivery.

Example:

Male harsh vocals with deep growls and high screams, aggressive
delivery.

Additional options may include:

• chant vocals • clean melodic vocals • shouted gang vocals

  --------------------------
  4. DEFINE SONG STRUCTURE
  --------------------------

Explicitly describing structure helps the AI generate more coherent
songs.

Example structure:

Intro Verse Chorus Verse Chorus Breakdown Final Chorus

  ---------------------------------
  5. DESCRIBE ENERGY AND DYNAMICS
  ---------------------------------

Define how the song’s energy changes between sections.

Example:

Verses tense and rhythmic. Chorus explosive and chantable. Breakdown
slow and crushing.

Dynamic descriptions help shape arrangement and pacing.

  -------------------
  6. ADD ATMOSPHERE
  -------------------

Optional but useful.

Atmosphere descriptors include:

• dark • cinematic • emotional • aggressive • atmospheric

Example:

Dark, intense, cinematic atmosphere.

  ----------------------------
  7. PROMPT LENGTH GUIDELINE
  ----------------------------

Effective Suno prompts typically range between 400 and 700 characters.

Avoid excessive instructions which may confuse the model.

Prompts must remain under 1000 characters.

  -------------------
  8. EXAMPLE PROMPT
  -------------------

Aggressive modern deathcore with crushing breakdowns and dark emotional
intensity. Downtuned chugging guitars, blast beats, heavy double kick
drums and distorted bass. Male harsh vocals with deep growls and high
screams, aggressive delivery with chant-style shouts in the chorus. Song
structure: intro, verse, chorus, verse, chorus, breakdown, final chorus.
Verses tense and rhythmic, chorus explosive and chantable, breakdown
slow and crushing. Dark, angry atmosphere with powerful dynamic shifts.

  ----------------------
  9. REUSABLE TEMPLATE
  ----------------------

[genre] with [tone]. [instrumentation]. [vocal style]. Song structure:
[structure]. Verses [energy description], chorus [energy], breakdown
[energy]. Atmosphere: [mood].

  ----------------
  CORE PRINCIPLE
  ----------------

A good prompt communicates the musical structure, instrumentation, vocal
delivery and emotional tone clearly while remaining concise.
