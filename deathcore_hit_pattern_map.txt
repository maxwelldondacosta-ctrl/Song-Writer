DEATHCORE HIT PATTERN MAP Song Writing Project – Pattern Library
Extension

  ----------
  OVERVIEW
  ----------

This document maps lyrical structures used by successful modern heavy
bands. The purpose is not imitation but pattern extraction. Deathcore
lyrics must survive:

• distorted guitars • blast beats / double kick • screamed or growled
vocals

Because of this environment, lyrical complexity is less important than
rhythm, punch phrases, and aggressive imagery.

  -----------------
  REFERENCE BANDS
  -----------------

Primary pattern references:

Lorna Shore – epic despair structures Knocked Loose – violent minimalism
Bring Me The Horizon – emotional collapse writing

These three represent different approaches to modern heavy music lyric
construction.

  ---------------------------------------
  1. LORNA SHORE PATTERN (EPIC DESPAIR)
  ---------------------------------------

Common structure:

cosmic imagery existential suffering collapse of hope catastrophic line

Example pattern:

The sky collapses The world decays Nothing survives I welcome the end

Language characteristics:

Apocalypse imagery: ruin, collapse, extinction

Darkness imagery: void, abyss, eternal night

Suffering imagery: torment, agony, despair

Purpose:

These words stretch well over long sustained screams.

  -----------------------------------------------
  2. KNOCKED LOOSE PATTERN (VIOLENT MINIMALISM)
  -----------------------------------------------

Typical structure:

short accusation short statement violent line breakdown phrase

Example:

You betrayed me You watched me bleed You let it happen Now watch me burn

Characteristics:

• extremely short lines • hard consonants • direct aggression

Primary goal:

crowd participation and rhythmic punch.

  ------------------------------------------------------
  3. BRING ME THE HORIZON PATTERN (EMOTIONAL COLLAPSE)
  ------------------------------------------------------

Typical structure:

self blame emotional pain confession dramatic statement

Example:

I tried to fix this I made it worse Every scar I hide Still drags me
under

Characteristics:

• emotional relatability • clearer phrasing • chorus driven writing

  -------------------------------
  BREAKDOWN ENGINEERING PATTERN
  -------------------------------

Most modern heavy songs follow this escalation system.

Stage 1 – Setup

You did this You made this

Stage 2 – Escalation

Everything burns Everything dies

Stage 3 – Drop Phrase

BURN BURN BURN

The drop phrase must be one or two words maximum. Anything longer gets
lost in the mix.

  ---------------------------
  DEATHCORE VOCABULARY BANK
  ---------------------------

These word groups repeatedly appear in successful heavy lyrics because
they punch through distorted mixes.

Destruction

burn break tear crush collapse

Decay

rot dust ash grave bones

Darkness

void abyss night shadow

Betrayal

parasite leech traitor liar

  --------------------------
  LINE LENGTH OPTIMIZATION
  --------------------------

Harsh vocals require shorter phrasing than rap or pop.

Typical ranges:

Punch phrase: 3–4 syllables Standard line: 5–8 syllables Extended
scream: 9–11 syllables

Longer lines tend to collapse rhythmically.

  --------------------------------
  HIGH PERFORMANCE HOOK PATTERNS
  --------------------------------

Loop Hook

You said forever You said forever You said forever Forever dies

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Rage Hook

You took everything You took everything Now choke on it

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Collapse Hook

Nothing remains Nothing remains Nothing remains

Repetition is common because screamed vocals prioritize rhythm over
linguistic complexity.

  ------------------------
  PATTERN SELECTION RULE
  ------------------------

When writing deathcore lyrics:

1.  Choose one primary emotional theme
2.  Choose one structural style

Example:

Epic Despair – Lorna Shore style Aggressive Confrontation – Knocked
Loose style Emotional Collapse – BMTH style

Mixing styles is possible but should be deliberate.
